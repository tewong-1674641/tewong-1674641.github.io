FAQ

What was the inspiration behind the name?
The name Wongderful Photography Studio came from a nickname that co-founder, Tyler Wong, was called. When the two friends were deciding on the name, the nickname was decided as the perfect name.

Do you offer pet sessions?
Yes, we do! While we don't list it on our site, you are more than welcome to ask. The prices may vary due to the addition of pet trainers being on site, but this adds to the experience.

What do you reccomend to beginning photographers?
Practice makes perfect! Several of our in-house photographers started at our studio. Following our mentorship program, our photographers were able to gain skill and confidence.

What is your first memory taking photos?
I was three years old and my father handed me a camera. I used to watch my father take photos of me and my siblings, so I kind of had an idea of how to take a photo. I don't remember doing this, but I started to take photos of my father. They weren't good photos, but it really inspired me to capture memories.
